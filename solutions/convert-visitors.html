    Identity resolution

    The moment of identity is not a starting line

    When a visitor finally identifies, through a signup, a login, or a form, most CDPs treat that as day one. Fanfinity treats it as a merge point. Every anonymous session, every behavioral signal, every inferred preference collapses into the persistent fan profile, so the fan graph is rich from the moment it becomes known.

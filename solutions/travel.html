01

The win-back use case

Turn a bad trip into a second chance, not a lost customer

A single poor experience is where travel loyalty quietly ends, and a satisfaction score is usually the first warning. When that score is connected to a traveler's booking and behavioral history under one profile, you can act on dissatisfaction early with a relevant gesture — instead of discovering the churn a season later, when they book with someone else.

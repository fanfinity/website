Real-time suppression

Every conversion should stop a spend, not extend it

When a fan converts, the worst thing you can do is keep spending to convert them again. Most platforms have a suppression lag of hours or a day, because suppression lists update in batch. Fanfinity's Reverse ETL pushes suppression updates the moment a fan profile changes, so a conversion at 2pm stops paid spend by 2pm, not 2am.

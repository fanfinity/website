Real-time lapse detection

The drift is visible long before the departure

A fan who is about to churn does not go from fully engaged to cancelled overnight. They drift: sessions get shorter, interactions get sparser, content gets ignored. Fanfinity tracks these signals in the fan profile in real time, so a lapse risk score is available the moment the pattern starts — not after the cancellation email arrives.

Win-back segmentation

Gone cold is not gone forever

Suppressed or lapsed fans are not lost forever. Audience Builder segments them by last active date, engagement history, and tier status — so a lapsed sports fan and a lapsed shopper get different offers, on the channels they actually answer.

Because segmentation runs on the same first-party fan graph that flagged the drift, win-back campaigns reach the right people with the right incentive — not a blanket discount sent to everyone who ever went quiet.

Under the hood

One platform behind every solution

Whichever vertical or team you're in, you're running the same five connected products, so collection, identity, engagement, activation, and measurement stay in sync.

  1. 01 · Collect

    Fanfinity Blocks

    Connect every signal.

    The CDI layer that collects events from every fan touchpoint, stitches identifiers in real time, enriches profiles for AI and activation, and distributes governed data to every tool in your stack.

    • Data Collection
    • Reverse ETL
    • Identity Stitching
    • AI Enrichment
    • Integrations

  2. 02 · Resolve

    Fanfinity ID

    Know every fan.

    A sovereign CDP and real-time identity graph that unifies every touchpoint (ticketing, streaming, app, web, on-site, social) into one golden fan profile, with consent governance built in.

    • Identity Resolution
    • Golden Record
    • Consent & Governance
    • Real-time Profiles

  3. 03 · Engage

    Fanfinity Engage Preview

    Engage every fan. (Preview)

    Live interactivity, gamification, and loyalty: polls, predictions, quizzes, quests, streaks, leaderboards, and sponsorable programs, all anchored to the same resolved fan profile. In design preview; not yet generally available.

    • Widgets & Programs
    • Gamification
    • Loyalty & Rewards
    • Custom Themes

  4. 04 · Activate

    Fanfinity Activate

    Reach every fan.

    The activation layer that turns one governed profile into real-time engagement across WhatsApp, email, push, and paid media. Segment on live behavior, orchestrate journeys that react, and suppress fans who've already converted.

    • Omnichannel Activation
    • Audience Segmentation
    • Journey Orchestration
    • Ad Audiences & Suppression

  5. 05 · Measure

    Fanfinity Measure

    Prove the value.

    Deterministic attribution and a sponsor/partner portal: verified reach instead of impressions, measurement from exposure through outcome, and premium audience monetization.

    • Verified Reach
    • Attribution
    • Sponsor Portal

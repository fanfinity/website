The opportunity

Your fans are watching. But are they engaged?

Clubs have millions of passionate supporters — yet most of that passion is one-way. Fans watch, scroll, and drift to other apps while the moments that matter pass by without them taking part. Every un-played poll, un-asked question and un-sponsored moment is loyalty, data and revenue left on the table.

Passive fans

  • Watch in silence, attention split across other screens
  • No record of who they are or what they care about
  • Engagement ends at the final whistle
  • Sponsor logos that fans tune out

Participating fans

  • Vote, predict, cheer and ask — in the moment
  • Every tap is first-party insight you own
  • Connected between matches and across the season
  • Sponsorable moments fans actually want to join

The product

What is Fanfinity Engage?

Fanfinity Engage is part of the products suite provided by Fanfinity, and the core product for engaging your fans. It turns your broadcasts, streams and pages into two-way experiences — interactive moments your fans take part in, in real time.

These moments are created as engagements. As this story progresses, more engagement types will be introduced — but they all share one goal: turn an audience into a community.

Why it matters for clubs

Five ways Engage grows your club

Engagement isn't a gimmick — it's a direct line to loyalty, data and revenue.

Deepen fan loyalty

Taking part creates belonging. Fans who play, predict and cheer feel like part of the club — so they stay longer and keep coming back.

Know your fans

Every interaction is first-party data you own — opinions, preferences and behaviour that sharpen marketing, content and ticketing decisions.

Open new revenue

Turn moments into inventory. Sponsor an engagement, brand a prediction, or deep-link fans to betting and commerce partners — engagement they choose to join.

Engage all year

Connection shouldn't stop at 90 minutes. Keep fans active between matches, through the off-season and across every channel you own.

Effortless to run

Produce engagements live during the action or schedule them ahead of kickoff. Themed to your brand, ready for web, app and stream.

Grow the fanbase

Shareable, social-ready moments give casual viewers a reason to join in — and a reason to bring friends with them.

The toolkit

The engagement library

A growing set of ready-to-use moments. Mix and match them across a match, a campaign or a season.

Polls

Loyalty · Data

Capture the crowd's voice.

Ask a question with no wrong answer and watch results update live, so every fan feels part of the crowd. Run text polls or image polls illustrated with player photos — and keep every opinion as insight.

Image Slider

Sentiment

Emotion on a scale.

Let fans react on a visual scale instead of a flat yes/no — a more expressive, emotional read on how the crowd really feels about a player, moment or decision.

Quizzes

Competition

Reward what your fans know.

Test their knowledge and reward it with points — gamified competition that turns club history and stats into a reason to keep playing and keep coming back.

Prediction

Retention

Create appointment moments.

Ask fans to call what happens next, then make them wait for the result. Guessing the outcome — and tuning back in to see if they were right — is a powerful reason to stay all the way to the final whistle.

Number Prediction

Retention

Make every stat a game.

When the answer is a number — final score, player goals, minutes to first goal — let fans predict it, then reveal who nailed it and who just missed.

Cheer Meter

Rivalry · Sponsorable

Turn rivalry into energy.

Fans tap as fast as they can to back their side. Tribal, loud and competitive — a natural home for a sponsor to power the head-to-head.

Alerts

Revenue

Own the moment — and monetize it.

Broadcast headlines, graphics, offers and commentary at exactly the right beat. Attach a link for sponsorships, ads, or a deep link into a partner app such as a betting platform.

Ask

Community

Give fans the mic.

Collect open-ended replies and feature the best ones. Crowd-source questions for on-air talent and let supporters see their own words on screen — community that fans help create.

Social Embeds

Reach

Bring the conversation in.

Pull live content from Twitter, Facebook or any oEmbed-supported provider straight into the experience — keeping the buzz inside your branded space instead of someone else's app.

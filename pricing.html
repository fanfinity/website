How pricing works

The four decisions, in detail

The composer above is not a toy version of the sales call — it is the sales call. Here is exactly how each decision maps to the commercial basis we put in writing.

  1. 01

    Pick your modules

    Start with fan identity and data, then add activation and measurement when the timing is right. You're billed for the modules you turn on, not a bundle you don't use yet.

  2. 02

    Priced by volume, not by headcount

    The basis is what you process, not how many fans you acquire or how many of them engage. Growing your audience and getting them active shouldn't inflate the bill. The exact volume basis is set in your contract.

  3. 03

    Enterprise needs are scoped in the same quote

    SLA, SSO, audit access, and deployment specifics are part of the conversation up front, not add-ons that appear at renewal. Specifics are scoped per contract.

  4. 04

    Talk to us for a quote

    We map the platform to your use case and volume, then put the number in writing before you sign. No metered surprises, no add-ons appearing at renewal.

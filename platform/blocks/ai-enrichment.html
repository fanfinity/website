The transformation

What enrichment actually adds

Three layers turn a noisy event log into something an AI can reason over — each one computed inside your environment, on data you own.

Layer 01 · Identity

Five records become one person

Events from the cookie, the inbox, the app, and the gate are deduplicated onto a single fan — so the model never counts one supporter as five strangers.

5 uids → Fan #48211

Layer 02 · Derivation

Behavior becomes attributes

Raw scans and sessions are distilled into derived attributes — matchday affinity, lapse risk, propensity to spend, lifecycle stage — that a model can use directly.

312 events → affinity: high · lapse risk: low

Layer 03 · Governance

Consent travels with context

Consent and residency flags are stamped onto the profile itself, so every answer an agent gives is already inside the rules — compliance is an input, not a review meeting.

consent: email ✓ push ✓ ads ✗

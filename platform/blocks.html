      What Blocks gives you

      Every signal.
      One governed pipeline.

      From collection to enrichment to delivery, Blocks connects your data stack so every downstream system works from the same resolved fan profile.

      1. 01 {% include icon.html name="plug" class="h-5 w-5" %}

        Data collection

        Ingest events from every fan touchpoint — web, app, ticketing, streaming, on-site, and offline sources — through an open connector framework. Raw payloads are normalized and validated as they arrive, so the data feeding your profiles is already clean before it reaches Fanfinity ID.

      2. 02 {% include icon.html name="identity" class="h-5 w-5" %}

        Identity stitching

        Match identifiers across devices, channels, and sessions as events land, so stitching runs in real time rather than as a nightly batch job. The resulting graph, its merge rules, and the governed profile live one layer up, in Fanfinity ID.

      3. 03 {% include icon.html name="spark" class="h-5 w-5" %}

        AI-ready enrichment

        Enrich every fan profile with first-party signals and derived attributes before they leave your environment, so downstream models and agents receive governed, structured context instead of raw event logs.

      4. 04 {% include icon.html name="stack" class="h-5 w-5" %}

        Reverse ETL

        Push governed fan data from your warehouse back to every operational tool in your stack. CRM, ad platforms, email, and support systems all receive the same resolved profile rather than a different export pieced together from each source.

      5. 05 {% include icon.html name="link" class="h-5 w-5" %}

        Integrations

        Connect your existing marketing, analytics, and data-science tooling through a programmable integration layer. Configuration is API-driven and version-controlled, so integrations behave like infrastructure you own, not a vendor console you depend on.

      How it works

      Collect, stitch, distribute

      Blocks sits at the base of the Fanfinity platform, normalizing raw signals into typed events, resolving identifiers in real time, and distributing governed data to every tool in your stack.

      1. 01

        Collect every signal

        Client-side SDKs and server-side connectors capture events across every touchpoint and deliver them into a typed, normalized event stream. No custom ETL to write, no parallel database to maintain alongside the sources you already run.

        →
      2. 02

        Stitch and resolve in real time

        Events are deduplicated and matched across identifiers as they arrive. The fan graph grows with every new signal, and every new resolution back-fills history across all linked identifiers, so profiles are always as complete as the data allows.

        →
      3. 03

        Distribute to the full stack

        Resolved, enriched profiles flow forward to Fanfinity ID for segmentation and downstream to every operational tool through reverse ETL. One governed source of truth, with no exports to re-sync between systems.

The honest read

A delivered engagement suite vs an owned fan graph

Monterosa has run interactive experiences for top-tier clubs, broadcasters, and entertainment shows for years. Votes, predictions, trivia, and gamified match centres are shipped, proven products with real broadcast-scale delivery behind them. On the engagement surface itself, we are not going to pretend otherwise.

One honesty note up front: our data and identity layer is live today; our live-engagement layer is a design Preview, not a shipped product. Monterosa's suite is delivered and in production. We say so plainly.

The question a serious evaluator asks is different: who owns the fan data those interactions create, where does it live, and what can you activate and prove with it afterwards. Fanfinity keeps the fan graph consented, first-party, and in-region (the layer you own), with activation and measurement built on top. Engagement is one way to feed it, not the container that holds it.
